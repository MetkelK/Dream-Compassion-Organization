Dream Compassion Organization

The purpose of The Dream Compassion Organization is to provide and assist any individuals who are in need of food, shelter, medical services, and/or emotional support services. The Dream Compassion Organization intends to form alliances and partnerships with existing organizations in the regions we are working in. The Dream Compassion Organization will also collaborate with the local communities in the regions we serve.

Contributions will be used to provide and assist any individuals who do not have access to, or are unable to access: food, shelter, medical services, and/or emotional support services. Our contributions will be targeted to individuals or communities who are vulnerable and/or in need; whether they be deprived of these four essential resources (food, shelter, medical services, and/or emotional support services), as a result of: war, conflict, poverty, gender based violence, trauma, or any circumstances out of their control.
